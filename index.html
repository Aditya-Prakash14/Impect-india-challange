Accessibility Technology

Indian Sign Language to Text/Speech Translation

Background

Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing communities in India face communication barriers in education, healthcare, and public services.

Problem

Develop a solution that can translate Indian Sign Language (ISL) into real-time text and speech output.

Expected Outcome

A user-friendly application (mobile/web) that enables seamless communication between the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing community and the hearing population.

Impact

This solution will empower millions of Indians with hearing disabilities, ensuring equal opportunities, accessibility, and social inclusion.

Hard
Computer Vision AI/ML Accessibility

Education Technology

Enhancing Education Access and Quality

Background

Despite the rapid growth of digital technologies, a significant portion of students across rural and semi-urban areas face challenges in accessing quality education.

Problem

How can we leverage technology to bridge the educational divide by making quality learning accessible, inclusive, and engaging?

Expected Outcome

A solution that provides accessible learning for students across different regions, backgrounds, and learning abilities.

Impact

Reduces learning gaps across socioeconomic divides and improves educational outcomes.

Medium
EdTech Web/Mobile UX Design

Agriculture Tech

Mobile App for Direct Market Access for Farmers

Background

Farmers in India face challenges in accessing real-time and reliable market information, often relying on intermediaries which reduces profit margins.

Problem

How can farmers get direct market access with daily updates and data-driven crop segregation information?

Expected Outcome

A digital solution that provides farmers with direct access to market platforms, ensuring transparency in pricing and enabling data-driven decisions.

Impact

Increases farmer income, reduces dependence on intermediaries, and enables better agricultural decisions.

Medium
Mobile App Data Analytics AgriTech
04
Healthcare Innovation

Predictive Model for Patient Deterioration

Background

In India's overcrowded hospitals, timely detection of patient deterioration is a major challenge due to limited staff and high patient loads.

Problem

How can technology assist healthcare providers in predicting patient deterioration in real-time, ensuring timely intervention?

Expected Outcome

A predictive system that identifies early warning signs of conditions like sepsis, cardiac arrest, and multi-organ failure.

Impact

Reduces preventable deaths through early detection and intervention in critical care situations.

Hard
Healthcare ML/AI Data Science

Education Technology

Smart Scholarship Navigator for Underprivileged Students

Background

Many rural and underprivileged students miss out on scholarships due to poor awareness and complex procedures.

Problem

How can students easily discover and apply for government/private scholarships relevant to their profiles?

Expected Outcome

An AI-powered scholarship portal that matches student profiles with available scholarships and guides step-by-step application in regional languages.

Impact

Boosts education access, minimizes dropouts, and supports talent in rural areas.

Medium
AI/ML Web Dev Education
06
Accessibility

Assistive Technology for Visually Impaired Students

Background

Blind and visually impaired students face challenges in accessing books, notes, and exams due to unavailability of inclusive tools.

Problem

How can we use technology to make study material and examinations accessible to visually impaired students?

Expected Outcome

An AI tool that converts text to audio, Braille, or haptic feedback and enables a secure speech-to-text exam system.

Impact

Equal education opportunities for visually impaired learners, reducing dependency on scribes.

Hard
Accessibility AI/ML Education

Rural Commerce

Digital Marketplace for Rural Artisans

Background

Rural artisans are unable to reach wider markets and get fair prices for their crafts.

Problem

How can artisans promote and sell products online without technical barriers?

Expected Outcome

A simple, multilingual web platform where artisans can list, advertise, and sell their work directly to consumers, with integrated payment solutions and AI-powered marketing tools.

Impact

Expands earning opportunities, preserves traditional arts, and enables economic empowerment.

Medium
E-commerce Web Dev Rural Tech
08
e-Governance

Rural e-Governance Service Navigator

Background

Villagers often remain unaware of government welfare schemes or find it difficult to apply due to complex procedures.

Problem

How can we provide rural citizens easy access to scheme information, eligibility checks, and application assistance in local languages?

Expected Outcome

A multilingual AI chatbot/web app that explains schemes, checks eligibility, and helps in filling and tracking applications.

Impact

This will increase awareness, reduce middlemen exploitation, and ensure government benefits reach the grassroots level.

Medium
NLP AI/ML Governance
