About the Event

Empowering India's brightest minds to solve real-world challenges

Transform Ideas into Impact

Rules & Guidelines

Fair play for maximum impact

Team Formation

Teams of 3-5 members. Cross-disciplinary collaboration encouraged.

Time Limit

7 days from problem release to final submission deadline.

Originality

All solutions must be original work created during the challenge.

Fair Play

Respect, collaboration, and ethical conduct are mandatory.

Documentation

Complete documentation and demo video required for submission.

Eligibility

Open to students and young professionals aged 18-28.
